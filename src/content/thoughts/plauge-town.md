This is a bit of a weird EP because overall I found that it was pretty mediocre and yet it also has hangar and southview on it, two fantastic songs that make it hard to not like plague town. Not to purposefully be too rude but the fact the same person that made hangar also made purgatory is just shocking. One of those songs was on repeat for the whole week after listening to this EP and the other was so boring I could barely sit through it. Perhaps it's partially Mr. oakscreen's fault (whoever that is) however that doesn't change the fact that a song this bad on a project this short is so lame and completely killed the vibes. The rest of the EP picked up afterward, especially with southview which I already mentioned which was catchy and had a pretty interesting sound to it, leaning a little more into guitar (or whatever it is). It's a great way to finish off the EP and the only reason it retained the 6.5.
