While I can't say I really enjoyed watching it, the characters are great and pretty well acted considering I've never even heard of most of the actors in this and the main villain was especially interesting, reminiscent of K from The Boxer (yes I know this came first but I read The Boxer first). Him being an evil obsessive asshole wasn't something you see super often and the actor playing him absolutely sells the performance.
